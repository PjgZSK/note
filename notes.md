- vector operations
    - v add(addition): 
        - graphic meaning
            - diagonal line
            - change length and direction
        - equation
            - (x1+x2, y1+y2)
    - v sub(subtraction)
        - graphic meaning
            - end-to-end line(from subtrahend to minuend)
            - change length and direction
        - equation
            - (x1-x2, y1-y2)
    - v scalar mul(multiplication)
        - graphic meaning
            - only change length
        - equation
            - (x*s, y*s)
    - v dot product
        - graphic meaning
            - projection
            - only scalar
        - equation
            - x1*x2+y1*y2
    - v cross product
        - graphic meaning
            - perpendicular vector
            - length equal area of the parallelogram
            - change length and direction
        - equation
            - (y1*z2-z1*y2, z1*x2-x1*z2, x1*y2-y1*x2)
